## Director-Level Strategic Requirements

As the Director of EEZ, the expectation for a new Head of Ecosystem Relations (HoER) is not "activity" but **category ownership**. The following five presentations are the mandatory deliverables for the first 21 days of the role.

### 1. The "Neutrality Gap" Audit (The Reality Check)
*   **Focus:** A brutal assessment of current market perception vs. internal vision.
*   **Key Output:** Identification of the "Gnosis Ghetto" risk and the "Forbidden Language" list that reinforces it.

### 2. The "TCP/IP" Category Design (The Battle Map)
*   **Focus:** Defining "One Ethereum" so clearly that competitors look like proprietary walled gardens.
*   **Key Output:** The "Sovereignty Matrix" (Tribes vs. The Zone) and the "Connectivity without Captivity" brand promise.

### 3. The Alliance "Wall of Content" Readiness Map
*   **Focus:** Gaining control over the 10 drifting Alliance members.
*   **Key Output:** The "Drift Scorecard" for each member and the coordinated 10-day content blitz schedule.

### 4. The "Sovereignty Validator" Pitch Deck (with EGL)
*   **Focus:** The narrative used to close the first non-Gnosis L2 partner.
*   **Key Output:** The economic case for atomic sync and the transparent timeline for Gnosis influence sunsetting.

### 5. The "War Room" Dashboard (The Execution Engine)
*   **Focus:** Providing the Director with high-signal, low-noise monitoring of the 60-day sprint.
*   **Key Output:** Leading indicators (Qualified conversations, AI-driven content audits) and the status of the June 1st Mandate.
