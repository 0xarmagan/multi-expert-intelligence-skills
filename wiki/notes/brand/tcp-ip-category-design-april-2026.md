## 1. The Sovereignty Matrix

The interop war is not about speed; it is about **who owns the network.**

| Category | Tribal Leaders (OP/Polygon) | The Neutral Zone (EEZ) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Model** | The "Captive" Tribe | The "Sovereign" Zone |
| **Logic** | "Join us and play by our rules." | "Connectivity without Captivity." |
| **Revenue** | Sequencer revenue share (tax). | Zero revenue share (utility). |
| **Switching Cost** | High (proprietary stack). | Low (open interop spec). |

## 2. The Brand Promise: Connectivity without Captivity

We don't sell "Interoperability." We sell **Sovereignty.**

*   **L2 Value Prop:** "Keep your brand, keep your sequencer revenue, and get atomic sync with the rest of Ethereum."
*   **Protocol Value Prop:** "Aave on Scroll can see collateral on Gnosis or Mainnet as if it's in the same block."
*   **The Moat:** Optimism and Polygon cannot copy this without abandoning their current architectures.

---

## 3. The Target: The "Independent L2s"

Our primary goal is to land a "Sovereignty Validator"—a non-Gnosis L2 that proves we are a neutral utility.

*   **Primary Targets:** Linea, Scroll, Mantle.
*   **The Reframe:** "Why be a sub-chain of a competitor when you can be a sovereign node in a neutral zone?"
*   **The Ask:** "One non-Gnosis L2 in committed evaluation by June 1."
