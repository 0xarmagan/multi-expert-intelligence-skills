# Role Definitions: Head of Ecosystem Relations vs. Ecosystem Growth Lead
**Date:** April 6, 2026 (revised)
**Tags:** HoER, EGL, roles, org-design, eez-gtm, task-registry
**Status:** Canonical — replaces prior version

---

## Framework
- **HoER:** Comms · Marketing · Brand · Positioning · Content
- **EGL:** Growth · DAO · Member Pipeline · CRM
- **Outreach:** Shared — EGL owns target + CRM, HoER owns message + approval

---

## HoER Task Ownership
### Brand & Positioning
- Messaging guide (canonical, maintained)
- Positioning guardian (reviews all public content before publication)
- Governance language sheet (Swiss non-profit language for all member posts)
- Brand standards enforcement (rejects off-message content)

### Content
- Alliance content brief (300–600 word "why we joined" template + distribution)
- Alliance draft review (approves or returns every member draft)
- Alliance publication calendar (simultaneous 10-day window coordination)
- Alliance member spotlight series (Phase 2, one/week)
- eez.io website copy (/alliance, /governance, /docs, homepage)
- @etheconomiczone social content (writes or approves all posts)
- EthResearch native posts (peer-participation tone, not marketing)
- Podcast & media content prep (talking points + framing for Ernst/Baylina)
- Press & earned media pitching (narrative angle, coordinates with Gnosis Comms)
- Co-marketing with protocol launches (brief, draft, review, publish)

### Alliance Member Relations
- Account management for 10 founding members (named contact, monthly call, engagement tracking)
- Content commitment extraction (written commitment + date from each member)
- Member onboarding (new members closed by EGL, onboarded by HoER)
- Alliance summit (content, speakers, narrative — joint with EGL on logistics)

### Reporting
- Weekly comms status (content in review, approved, published, off-message incidents)
- Alliance member engagement tracker (lightweight, separate from CRM)

---

## EGL Task Ownership
### Growth & Pipeline
- Non-Gnosis L2 operator pipeline (hard gate: 1 committed evaluation by June 1)
- New protocol integration pipeline (beyond founding 10)
- New Alliance member growth (net-new members, quarterly targets)
- EF & researcher relations (flag alignment risks to HoER)
- ETHGlobal hackathon bounties (budget, design, DevRel coordination)
- Non-EVM chain outreach (Phase 2)
- Phase gate tracking + escalation (dashboard, red flag escalation to leadership)

### DAO
- DAO governance strategy (which forums, what order, when)
- DAO forum posts — draft per forum, customized, submit to HoER for messaging review before posting
- DAO forum ongoing engagement (replies, technical Q&A with DevRel)
- DAO relationship management (delegates, contributors in CRM)

### CRM & Operations
- Full CRM ownership (L2 operators, protocol teams, DAOs, researchers, new Alliance prospects)
- Pipeline reporting (weekly to EEZ leadership)

---

## Outreach (Shared)
EGL: target identification, first contact, CRM logging
HoER: message template, tone, approval

| Type | EGL | HoER |
|---|---|---|
| New L2 operator cold outreach | Target + send + CRM | Message template + approval |
| New protocol team outreach | Target + send + CRM | Message template + approval |
| Alliance member re-engagement | Flags drifting members (CRM) | Sends + owns conversation |
| Event outreach | Logistics, booth, hackathon | Content, materials, talk briefing |
| Media / podcast outreach | Target identification | Pitch writing + framing + spokesperson prep |
