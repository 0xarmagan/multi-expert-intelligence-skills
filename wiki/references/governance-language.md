# EEZ Governance Language Sheet

The following language MUST be included or reflected in all content regarding the Ethereum Economic Zone (EEZ).

## Approved Governance Block
> "The Ethereum Economic Zone (EEZ) is governed by the EEZ Association, an independent, Swiss non-profit foundation. The Association’s mandate is to maintain and grow the EEZ as a public, neutral infrastructure for the Ethereum ecosystem. While Gnosis provided the initial technical research and funding, the EEZ is an open, member-governed zone that is not controlled by any single protocol or entity."

## Key Governance Talking Points
- **Neutrality:** EEZ is a neutral layer for all rollups, not a Gnosis-centric ecosystem.
- **Independence:** The EEZ Association is the canonical governing body.
- **Transparency:** All governance decisions are made in the open, as documented at `eez.io/governance`.
- **Public Infrastructure:** EEZ is designed as a public good for the Ethereum ecosystem.

## Usage Guidelines
- **Alliance Member Posts:** Every "Why we joined" post must include the governance block.
- **Media Appearances:** Ernst and Baylina should lead with the Swiss non-profit structure to differentiate EEZ from "a Gnosis product."
- **Social Media:** Use "governed by the EEZ Association" in threads that describe the project's vision.
