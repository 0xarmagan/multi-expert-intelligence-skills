## Current Synthesis
The **EEZ (Ethereum Economic Zone)** is shifting from a Gnosis-led infrastructure project to a **Sovereign Interop Layer** for the broader Ethereum ecosystem. The core technical advantage — **synchronous composability (atomic sync)** — allows transactions to execute across multiple L2s in a single 12-second block. This positions EEZ as the "TCP/IP of Ethereum" — a neutral protocol for "Connectivity without Captivity."

The strategic focus for Q2 2026 is **Structural Proof of Neutrality (SPoN)**. To win the "Interop War," EEZ must move from being perceived as a "Gnosis product" to a neutral industry standard. This requires a non-Gnosis L2 partner commitment by June 1, 2026.
