# EEZ Alliance Member Content Brief

## Goal
A "why we joined" post of 300–600 words that signals your commitment to the EEZ and explains the value proposition to your protocol’s community.

## Content Requirements
1. **The Problem:** What specific problem was your protocol facing (e.g., bridge risk, liquidity fragmentation) that EEZ addresses?
2. **Synchronous Composability:** What does "One Ethereum" and synchronous composability concretely unlock for your users?
3. **The Why:** Why did your protocol join the EEZ Alliance now?
4. **Governance:** Mention the EEZ Association as the governing entity (see below).
