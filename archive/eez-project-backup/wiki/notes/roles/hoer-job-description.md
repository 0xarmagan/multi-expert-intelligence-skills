# Head of Ecosystem Relations
**Organisation:** EEZ Association · Swiss non-profit · EF co-funded
**Type:** Full-time · Remote

---

## About EEZ

EEZ (Ethereum Economic Zone) enables atomic transactions across multiple L2s and Ethereum mainnet within a single 12-second block — eliminating fragmentation across the $40B L2 ecosystem. We are in the critical window between first technical demonstration and first network-effects-viable deployment. Category leaders are made here.

---

## The Role

You own EEZ's brand strategy, positioning framework, and Alliance partner relationships. Gnosis has a Head of Marketing and a Head of Content — they execute. You set direction, own the relationships, and hold the line on what EEZ stands for.

Your output is an ecosystem of credible, engaged, on-message Alliance partners and a positioning framework strong enough to survive technically sophisticated audiences. Before mainnet, that credibility is EEZ's only asset.

---

## Responsibilities

**Brand Strategy & Positioning**
- Own and maintain the EEZ messaging guide — category framing, approved language, forbidden phrases, segment variants
- Set positioning strategy per segment: Protocol Teams, L2 Operators, Alliance Members, Researchers, DAOs
- Brief Gnosis Marketing and Content before any campaign, sprint, or channel activation
- Hold final approval on all external content — social, blog, press, Alliance member posts, DAO forum posts — before publication

**Ecosystem Building & Partner Relations**
- Named strategic contact for all 10 founding Alliance members — monthly calls, engagement tracking, re-engagement when relationships drift
- Define what Alliance membership means in practice: what partners commit to, what EEZ delivers
- Onboard new partners once Ecosystem Growth Lead closes them
- Co-own EF and researcher relations on positioning-sensitive matters
- Set co-marketing strategy for protocol launches; brief Gnosis Content on execution

**Internal Interface**
- Write positioning briefs that drive Gnosis Marketing and Content's execution
- Brief Ernst and Baylina before external appearances — approved framing, what not to say
- Sync with Ecosystem Growth Lead weekly on pipeline, new partner handoffs, and outreach alignment

---

## What You Don't Own

Content production, press relationships, campaign execution, and channel management belong to Gnosis Marketing and Content. Growth pipeline, new member recruitment, DAO governance strategy, and CRM belong to the Ecosystem Growth Lead. You brief and approve; you do not produce or prospect.

---

## Who You Are

- Senior background in ecosystem, partnership, or brand strategy in a technical or crypto-native context
- Can distinguish a positioning problem from a perception problem — and knows the remedy is different for each
- Comfortable with authority without execution: you brief well, hold quality bars without doing the work yourself, and give other teams what they need to produce the right output
- Can tell a founding Alliance partner their draft needs rewriting — and keep the relationship intact
- Can read a technical claim and determine whether it is verified or speculative; you are the last check before unverified statements publish under EEZ's name

---

## Why It Matters

One off-message post from a founding Alliance member is a permanent search result. One unverified benchmark cited as a specification is a credibility incident with EthResearch and ZK Podcast audiences that cannot be walked back. This role exists to prevent both — not by controlling every word, but by building a framework clear enough that the right words come naturally.
